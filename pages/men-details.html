Welcome to Mens Clinic International.

Of all the conditions men face, sexual problems are the most personal. Although they are very common, they are not inevitable or permanent, and today there are many modern treatments available.No matter how long a man has been experiencing a problem, it can be treated successfully. Therefore the purpose of this website is to provide a better understanding of male sexual dysfunction, why it occurs and what treatment options are now available.

Men’s Clinic International deals with sexual problems that are related to:

Weak Erections

Weak Erections (also known as impotence or erectile dysfunction) are not a disease, but a secondary condition brought on by other, primary causes.

Early Ejaculation

Early ejaculation (also known as premature ejaculation) is simply defined as “ejaculation occurring before the individual wishes it”.

Low Libido

Low libido can loosely be defined as “a lack of sexual desire”. In other words low libido describes a decreased interest in sexual activity.

STIs

Sexually transmitted diseases constitute a major health problem in Southern Africa.
Significantly, over one million people seek treatment for Sexually transmitted diseases each year.
